# R&D Institute

The first of the Syndicate's two difficulty levers. The R&D Institute is **capital**: you stake **$PROM** to fund the Syndicate's research, and in return you're issued **better tools** — which lower your personal mining difficulty by up to **3x**.

## How it works

1. Stake **$PROM** into the R&D Institute on Solana.
2. The oracle reads your stake and reports it to Promethium Chain.
3. Your mining difficulty drops, scaled to your stake, capped at **3x easier**.
4. While your stake stays in, your tools stay sharp.

## Good to know

- **It's personal.** Your discount applies to your mining only — it doesn't change anyone else's.
- **Cap is 3x.** Beyond a point the discount stops climbing; everyone hits the same ceiling.
- **It stacks with labour.** The R&D bonus (tools, up to 3x) combines with the Recruitment Office bonus (labour, up to 2x). See **Recruitment Office**.
- **Optional.** You can always mine at full difficulty with no stake.
- Staking / unstaking each cost **1 USDC** via x402. See **Fees & x402**.

## Tools x labour

Think of mining output as `tools x labour`. The R&D Institute upgrades your tools; the Recruitment Office adds hands. Fund both and you mine the cheapest.
