# Promethium & Decay

Promethium is gorgeous and impatient. Mining it is the easy part. *Keeping* it is the game.

## Phase 1 — The Haul (safe)

Fresh from a mined block, promethium needs **100 blocks** to reach the surface. While it's climbing:

- nothing decays — your stack is fully protected
- you can't move or stabilize it yet

Depth protects. Think of these 100 blocks as the calm before the clock.

## Phase 2 — The Surface (the clock is running)

The instant promethium surfaces, it's exposed — and decay begins. **Half-life: 17.7 hours.**

```
   surfaced -17.7h-> 1/2 -17.7h-> 1/4 -17.7h-> 1/8 -> ... -> gone
```

It's not a cliff — it's a slope. You don't lose everything at hour 17.7; you lose *half*. Then half of the rest. Every hour you wait costs you. The decay is gentle but it never sleeps.

## Phase 3 — Stabilize (the split)

You always run surfaced promethium through the **Stabilization Plant**. How much survives depends only on how fast you got there:

- **On time** -> the whole batch stabilizes into **$PROM**.
- **Late** -> you keep what survived as **$PROM**; the decayed slice settles into the **Relief Fund** and pays stakers.

```
   stabilize fast  ->  ~100% $PROM   |  ~0% lost
   stabilize late  ->  less  $PROM   |  the rest to the Relief Fund
```

You never lose everything — you lose a *slice*, and the slice grows the longer you sit. Promethium rewards the quick and quietly redistributes from the slow.

> **Pro move:** stabilize the moment it surfaces. Every hour at the surface is a slice handed to the Relief Fund instead of to you. Better yet, let an agent do it the second it's ready — see **Agentic Mining**.
