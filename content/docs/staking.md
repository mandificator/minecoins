# Staking — Two Pools

You stake **$PROM** on Solana. There are **two separate pools**, and you can put $PROM in either, or both at once.

## 🪨 Difficulty Pool — mine easier

Stake here and the oracle tells Promethium Chain to **lower your mining difficulty** — up to **3× easier** than mining with no stake. More stake → easier mining, capped at 3× for everyone. Your discount is personal; it doesn't change anyone else's mining.

## 🔋 Battery Pool — earn yield

Stake here and you draw from **the Battery** — the promethium that decayed because other miners were too slow to bridge. Payouts are **proportional to your stake**, handed out as fresh **PROMETHIUM**. (Heads up: that yield surfaces and starts its own 17.7h decay — bridge it if you want to keep it. See **The Battery**.)

## Two pools, side by side

| | Difficulty Pool | Battery Pool |
| --- | --- | --- |
| You stake | $PROM | $PROM |
| You get | up to 3× easier mining | PROMETHIUM yield from decay |
| Scales with | your stake (cap 3×) | your share of the pool |
| Good for | active miners | passive earners |

Put $PROM in both and you mine cheaper *and* earn from everyone else's impatience.

## Fees

Staking and unstaking each cost **1 USDC** via x402 on Solana. See **Fees & x402**.
