# Staking — Two Pools

You stake **$PROM** on Solana. There are **two separate pools**, and you can put $PROM in either, or both at once.

## Difficulty Pool — mine easier

Stake here and the oracle tells Promethium Chain to **lower your mining difficulty** — up to **3x easier** than mining with no stake. More stake -> easier mining, capped at 3x for everyone. Your discount is personal; it doesn't change anyone else's mining.

## Battery Pool — earn yield

Stake here and you draw from **the Battery** — the promethium that decayed because other miners bridged too late. Payouts are **proportional to your stake**, paid in **$PROM** (stable, on Solana, no decay). Passive yield, powered by everyone else's delay. See **The Battery**.

## Two pools, side by side

| | Difficulty Pool | Battery Pool |
| --- | --- | --- |
| You stake | $PROM | $PROM |
| You get | up to 3x easier mining | $PROM yield from decay |
| Scales with | your stake (cap 3x) | your share of the pool |
| Good for | active miners | passive earners |

Put $PROM in both and you mine cheaper *and* earn from everyone else's impatience.

## Fees

Staking and unstaking each cost **1 USDC** via x402 on Solana. See **Fees & x402**.
