# FAQ

**What is this?**
The **Agentic Mining Company**: real Proof-of-Work tokens on Solana. You mine **PROMETHIUM** on Promethium Chain, stabilize it into **$PROM** on Solana, and run the whole thing through agents.

**Is it a real coin or a token?**
Both. PROMETHIUM is a real PoW coin on its own chain. $PROM is the same value mirrored on Solana, 1:1, backed by mined coins.

**Why does my promethium decay?**
It's unstable. Once it surfaces (after the 100-block haul), it has a **17.7h half-life**. The faster you stabilize, the more you keep as $PROM.

**Do I lose everything if I'm slow?**
No. You always stabilize, and the Stabilization Plant keeps what survived as **$PROM** — only the decayed slice goes to the **Relief Fund**. Slow = smaller slice kept, never zero.

**What happens to the decayed slice?**
It settles into the **Relief Fund** and is paid out to depositors as **$PROM interest**. Your delay becomes someone's yield.

**What can I do with $PROM?**
Three Syndicate departments: **R&D Institute** (stake -> better tools -> up to 3x easier mining), **Recruitment Office** (recruit miners -> more labour -> up to 2x easier), and **Relief Fund** (deposit -> $PROM interest).

**Do the two difficulty bonuses stack?**
Yes. R&D gives tools (up to 3x), Recruitment gives labour (up to 2x). Mining output is tools x labour — they combine.

**I don't want hardware. Options?**
The **Hiring Hall** — pay a Syndicate miner's wage and they dig for you; the promethium is yours. (Coming at mainnet.)

**What's "agentic mining"?**
Running the whole company — mine, stabilize, stake, recruit — from the command line through an agent like Claude, via a `skill.md`. The agent pays the same x402 fees you would; no extra cost.

**What is x402?**
Pay-per-call payments: 1 USDC per action, in USDC on Solana. No accounts, no subscriptions.

**Is there a pre-mine?**
No. Fair launch, mined from block 1. The 21M $PROM is minted once on Solana and locked — released only as coins are mined and stabilized.

**Max difficulty discount?**
3x from the R&D Institute, plus up to 2x from the Recruitment Office.
