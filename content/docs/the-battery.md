# The Battery

Here's the twist that makes Promethium more than a mining coin.

When promethium decays at the surface, it **doesn't disappear**. The decayed slice is captured at the Bridge and drained into **the Battery**, a reservoir living on Promethium Chain. Fitting, really: in the real world, promethium is literally used to make nuclear batteries.

## Where the charge comes from

Every miner who sits too long before bridging loses a slice to decay — and that slice **charges the Battery**. Their delay becomes the Battery's gain.

```
   slow miners' decayed slice --> [ THE BATTERY ] --> paid out as $PROM
                                                      to the Battery Pool
```

## Where the charge goes

The Battery pays out to people who **stake $PROM in the Battery Pool**. Your share is proportional to how much you've staked. The payout is **$PROM** — stable, on Solana, no decay. It's passive yield, powered entirely by the impatience of others.

## In short

- Bridge late? Your decayed slice feeds the Battery.
- Stake in the Battery Pool? You drink from it, in stable $PROM.
- Either way, no promethium is ever destroyed — it just changes hands.
