# Recruitment Office

**We're hiring.** The second difficulty lever — and this one is **labour**.

Promethium is brutal to mine alone. Bring other miners into the Syndicate under you, and the company issues you **better gear** for building the workforce. The more miners you recruit, the **lower your mining difficulty** — up to **2x easier**.

## How it works

1. Invite miners to the Syndicate with your referral.
2. As your crew grows, the Recruitment Office issues you upgraded equipment.
3. Your mining difficulty drops, scaled to your crew, capped at **2x easier**.

## It stacks with R&D

The two difficulty levers are complementary, not redundant — one gives **tools**, the other gives **labour**, and mining output is `tools x labour`:

```
   R&D Institute       -> better TOOLS   -> up to 3x easier   (stake $PROM)
   Recruitment Office  -> more LABOUR    -> up to 2x easier   (recruit miners)
   ----------------------------------------------------------
   Stacked             -> dig faster on both fronts
```

Better tools, more hands. The Syndicate rewards capital and labour alike.
