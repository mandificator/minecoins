# Faucet

The Promethium faucet gives out free **PROM** to real people in the community. Connect
your X account, make a genuine post about Promethium, drop your PROM address, and the
payout arrives automatically. Live at [promethium.work/faucet](/faucet).

## Who can claim

- An X account that is **≥ 90 days old** with **≥ 100 followers** (checked automatically on connect).
- **One claim per X account**, and **one PROM address per account**. The limit is tied to
  your account's identity, not your handle — renaming won't reset it.

## How much

| Tier | Reward |
|---|---|
| Regular X account | **1 PROM** |
| Verified account | **3 PROM** |
| Claimed with a referral code | **+0.1 PROM** on top |

## How to claim

1. **Connect X.** We verify age + followers automatically.
2. **Post about Promethium — in your own words.** Please don't copy-paste a canned
   message (X filters duplicate spam, and unique posts reach more people). A good post:
   - mentions **@promethium_work**
   - says **why you like the chain** / what makes it different
   - shares your **bullish take and ideas** for the project

   The "Open X" button opens the composer already tagging **@promethium_work** with the
   link — just add your own thoughts.
3. **Drop your address.** Paste your `prom1…` Promethium address. Don't have one yet? See
   [Get Started](/docs/get-started). Optionally enter a **referral code** here for +0.1 PROM.
4. **Get paid.** Once your claim is recorded, the payout is sent **automatically** (batched
   roughly every half hour). Keep that wallet — that's where your PROM lands.

## Referrals

When you claim, you're assigned your **own referral code**, shown on the confirmation
screen (copy it there). Share it:

- Every friend who claims with your code earns **you +0.5 PROM**.
- They get **+0.1 PROM** extra on top of their normal reward.

There's no cap — the more people you bring who genuinely claim, the more you earn.

## Notes

- Payouts are automatic and de-duplicated: you can never be paid twice for the same claim.
- The faucet is community-funded — anyone can top it up by sending PROM to the faucet
  address shown on the [explorer](/explorer).
