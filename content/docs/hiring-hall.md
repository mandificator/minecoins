# Hiring Hall

Don't want to run hardware at all? The Hiring Hall lets you put a **Promethium Mining Syndicate** miner to work for you.

You pay the miner's **wage**; they mine on your behalf; the **PROMETHIUM they dig is yours.** No rig, no setup, no heat in your living room.

```
   You -> pay wage -> [ PMS miner digs ] -> PROMETHIUM to you
```

## How it works

1. Hire a miner from the Hiring Hall and fund their wage.
2. The miner runs real Proof-of-Work on Promethium Chain for you.
3. The promethium they find is credited to you — then it's yours to stabilize like any other (mind the 17.7h clock once it surfaces).

## Good to know

- You're paying for **labour you don't have to run** — the mined promethium still surfaces and decays like normal, so stabilize it (or let your agent do it).
- Pairs naturally with **Agentic Mining**: hire a miner *and* have an agent stabilize the output the moment it surfaces.
