# Glossary

**Agentic Mining Company** — what we are: real Proof-of-Work tokens on Solana, run by agents.

**Promethium Mining Syndicate (PMS)** — the company / foundation. Its departments run staking, stabilizing, referrals, and hire.

**Promethium Chain** — the blockchain where promethium is mined. A Bitcoin-codebase fork.

**PROMETHIUM** — the real Proof-of-Work coin mined on Promethium Chain (the coin side).

**$PROM** — the token on Solana, backed 1:1 by mined promethium (the token side).

**Decay** — surfaced promethium's 17.7h half-life. The longer you wait to stabilize, the bigger the slice you lose.

**The Haul** — the 100 blocks it takes mined promethium to reach the surface. Safe; no decay.

**The Surface** — where promethium becomes stabilizable... and starts decaying.

**The Stabilization Plant** — the bridge. Decants surfaced PROMETHIUM into $PROM; the decayed slice settles into the Relief Fund. One-way, agentic.

**R&D Institute** — stake $PROM to fund research and get better tools. Difficulty lever (tools), up to 3x easier.

**Recruitment Office** — recruit miners for better gear. Difficulty lever (labour), up to 2x easier.

**Relief Fund** — deposit $PROM, earn $PROM interest from captured decay. The yield department.

**Hiring Hall** — pay a PMS miner's wage; they mine for you, the promethium is yours.

**Oracle** — reads your Solana stakes/crew and tells the chain your discounts and your interest share.

**Agentic mining** — running the whole company via CLI through an agent (Claude) using a `skill.md`.

**OP_RETURN** — data attached to a PROMETHIUM transfer; carries your Solana address when stabilizing manually.

**x402** — pay-per-call payments. 1 USDC per action, in USDC on Solana. Agents pay the same — no extra.

**Fair launch** — no pre-mine, no insiders. All coins mined from block 1.
